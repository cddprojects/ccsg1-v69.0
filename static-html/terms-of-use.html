Terms of Use

Last updated: May 12, 2026

These terms describe the general use of the Link Roles website, application forms, role-category information pages, and related applicant support services.

Platform role

Link Roles provides general information and an online application pathway for individuals exploring remote-friendly role categories shared by third-party hiring companies. Current role categories may include remote admin support, customer support, online merchant support, e-commerce support, online sales support, and AI content support. Link Roles is generally not the employer, recruiter, payroll provider, or hiring decision maker unless expressly stated.

No guarantee

Submitting an application through this website does not guarantee interviews, job placement, role availability, compensation, income, or any employment outcome.

Third-party hiring companies

Any role details, eligibility checks, pay terms, interviews, contracts, onboarding, verification, and work arrangements are determined by the relevant third-party hiring company.

Applicant responsibility

Applicants should review opportunity details carefully, provide accurate information, and exercise discretion before sharing sensitive information or accepting any work arrangement.

Website content

Content on this website is provided for general information about remote-friendly role categories and the application process. It may be updated, corrected, or removed without notice.

Contact

For questions, contact support@link-roles.com.
